# VibeCurator Design System

## Brand Identity
Personal designer in your pocket. Calm, premium, playful. Indian home meets modern minimal.

## Color Palette

### Light Mode (Default)
- `--bg-primary`: #FAFAF7 (soft white)
- `--bg-secondary`: #F5F0E8 (warm cream)
- `--bg-card`: #FFFFFF
- `--accent`: #E8956D (warm terracotta)
- `--accent-soft`: #FFF0E8
- `--text-primary`: #1A1714
- `--text-secondary`: #6B6560
- `--text-muted`: #A8A29E
- `--border`: #EDE9E4
- `--shadow`: rgba(26, 23, 20, 0.08)

### Dark Mode
- `--bg-primary`: #18161A
- `--bg-secondary`: #221F24
- `--bg-card`: #2C2830
- `--accent`: #E8956D
- `--text-primary`: #F5F0E8
- `--text-secondary`: #A89F9B
- `--border`: #3A3540

## Typography
- **Font**: Poppins (400, 500, 600, 700)
- **Display**: 28px, weight 700, tight letter-spacing
- **Title**: 20px, weight 600
- **Body**: 15px, weight 400, line-height 1.6
- **Caption**: 12px, weight 500, uppercase tracking

## Spacing
- Base unit: 4px
- Content padding: 20px
- Card padding: 16px
- Section gap: 24px

## Components

### Sticker Cards
- White background, 12px border radius
- Shadow: 0 4px 16px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)
- Slightly rotated (-2° to 2°) for playfulness

### Recommendation Cards
- Full-width, 16px radius
- Before/after slider overlay
- Match score badge: accent pill top-right

### Vibe Chips
- Pill shape, 20px radius
- Default: cream bg, muted text
- Active: accent bg, white text

### Budget Slider
- Accent thumb color
- Warm track gradient

## Motion
- Screen transitions: slide from right
- Sticker reveal: staggered fade-up
- Card entrance: spring animation
